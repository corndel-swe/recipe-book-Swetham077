Starters

Patatas Bravas with Garlic Aioli

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ pounds Fingerling Potatoes
  • 5 tablespoons Olive Oil
  • Sea Salt and Pepper
  • Fresh Thyme and Parsley
  • Spanish Paprika
  • 3 Garlic Cloves (pressed)
  • ½ cup High-Quality Mayonnaise
  • 1 ½ tablespoons Fresh Lemon Juice

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Cut the potatoes into bite-sized pieces and toss with olive oil, salt, pepper, thyme, and paprika.
  3. Spread the potatoes on a baking sheet and roast for 20-25 minutes until crispy.
  4. For the aioli, mix the pressed garlic, mayonnaise, and lemon juice.
  5. Serve the potatoes with the garlic aioli on the side.

Calamares a la Romana (Spanish Fried Calamari)

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound Calamari Tubes (cleaned and cut into rings)
  • 1 cup All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 teaspoon Baking Powder
  • 1 teaspoon Salt
  • 1 teaspoon Smoked Paprika
  • 1 cup Sparkling Water (cold)
  • Vegetable Oil (for frying)
  • Lemon Wedges (for serving)

Instructions:

  1. Clean the calamari tubes and cut them into rings.
  2. In a bowl, mix the flour, baking powder, salt, and smoked paprika.
  3. Gradually add the cold sparkling water, whisking until you have a smooth batter.
  4. Dip the calamari rings into the batter, ensuring they are well coated.
  5. Heat the vegetable oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 350°F (175°C).
  6. Fry the calamari in batches until golden brown and crispy, about 2-3 minutes per batch.
  7. Remove with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels.
  8. Serve the crispy calamari hot with lemon wedges on the side.

Jalapeño Poppers

Ingredients:

  • 12 Jalapeños
  • 6 ounces Cream Cheese (softened)
  • 1 teaspoon Garlic Powder
  • 4 ounces Sharp Cheddar Cheese (shredded)
  • 2 tablespoons Fresh Chives (chopped)
  • ¼ cup Panko Bread Crumbs
  • 1 tablespoon Melted Butter

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Cut the jalapeños in half lengthwise and remove the seeds and membranes.
  3. In a bowl, mix the cream cheese, garlic powder, cheddar cheese, and chives until well combined.
  4. Spoon the cheese mixture into each jalapeño half, filling them generously.
  5. In a small bowl, mix the panko bread crumbs with melted butter until well coated.
  6. Sprinkle the bread crumb mixture over the stuffed jalapeños.
  7. Place the stuffed jalapeños on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  8. Bake for 20-25 minutes until the jalapeños are tender and the topping is golden brown.
  9. Let them cool slightly before serving.

Main Courses

Paella

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons Olive Oil
  • 1 Onion (chopped)
  • 1 Red Bell Pepper (chopped)
  • 3 Garlic Cloves (minced)
  • 2 cups Arborio Rice
  • 1 teaspoon Saffron Threads
  • 1 teaspoon Smoked Paprika
  • 4 cups Chicken Broth
  • 1 cup White Wine
  • 1 pound Shrimp (peeled and deveined)
  • 1 pound Mussels (cleaned)
  • 1 cup Frozen Peas
  • 1 Lemon (cut into wedges)
  • Fresh Parsley (chopped, for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large paella pan or skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the onion and red bell pepper, and sauté until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Stir in the garlic and cook for another minute.
  4. Add the Arborio rice, saffron threads, and smoked paprika, stirring to coat the rice with the oil and spices.
  5. Pour in the chicken broth and white wine, and bring to a simmer.
  6. Reduce the heat to low and let the rice cook, uncovered, for about 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. Add the shrimp, mussels, and frozen peas, and cook until the seafood is cooked through and the mussels have opened, about 5-7 minutes.
  8. Remove from heat and let the paella rest for a few minutes.
  9. Garnish with fresh parsley and lemon wedges before serving.

Drinks

Sangria

Ingredients:

  • 1 bottle Red Wine
  • 1 Orange (sliced)
  • 1 Lemon (sliced)
  • 2 tablespoons Sugar
  • 1 shot Brandy
  • 2 cups Soda Water
  • Ice
  • Fresh Mint (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. In a large pitcher, combine the red wine, orange slices, lemon slices, and sugar.
  2. Stir in the brandy and soda water.
  3. Add ice and stir well.
  4. Garnish with fresh mint before serving.

Agua de Valencia

Ingredients:

  • 1 bottle Cava (Spanish sparkling wine)
  • 1 cup Fresh Orange Juice
  • 1 shot Vodka
  • 1 shot Gin
  • 2 tablespoons Sugar
  • Ice
  • Orange Slices (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. In a large pitcher, combine the cava, orange juice, vodka, gin, and sugar.
  2. Stir well and add ice.
  3. Garnish with orange slices before serving.

Tinto de Verano

Ingredients:

  • 1 bottle Red Wine
  • 1 bottle Lemon-Lime Soda
  • Ice
  • Lemon Slices (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. In a large pitcher, combine the red wine and lemon-lime soda.
  2. Add ice and stir well.
  3. Garnish with lemon slices before serving.

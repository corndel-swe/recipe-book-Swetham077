Gazpacho (Cold Tomato Soup) Recipe

Ingredients

  • 6 ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cucumber, peeled and chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 small red onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 cups tomato juice
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a blender, combine tomatoes, cucumber, bell pepper, onion, and garlic. Blend until smooth.
  2. Add tomato juice, olive oil, and red wine vinegar. Blend again until well combined.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours before serving.
  4. Garnish with fresh basil before serving.

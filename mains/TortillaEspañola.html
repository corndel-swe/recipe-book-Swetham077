Tortilla Española (Spanish Omelette) Recipe

Ingredients

  • 6 large eggs
  • 3 medium potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add potatoes and onion, and cook until tender.
  2. In a bowl, beat the eggs and season with salt and pepper. Add the cooked potatoes and onion to the eggs.
  3. Pour the mixture back into the pan and cook on low heat until the eggs are set. Flip the omelette to cook the other side.
  4. Once cooked, slide the omelette onto a plate and let it cool slightly before serving.

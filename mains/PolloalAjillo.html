Pollo al Ajillo (Garlic Chicken) Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 8 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add chicken pieces and cook until browned on all sides.
  2. Add minced garlic and cook for another 2 minutes.
  3. Pour in white wine and chicken broth. Add bay leaf and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 30 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.
  5. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

Paella Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 cups short-grain rice
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp saffron threads
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup peas
  • 1 lb seafood mix (shrimp, mussels, clams)
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add onion and garlic, and saut√© until softened.
  2. Add red and green bell peppers, and cook for another 5 minutes.
  3. Stir in the rice, smoked paprika, and saffron threads. Cook for 2 minutes, stirring constantly.
  4. Pour in the chicken broth and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Add peas and seafood mix. Cover and cook for another 10 minutes, or until the seafood is cooked through and the rice is tender.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with fresh parsley and lemon wedges before serving.
